Stay Safe Out There!

Safety is always top of mind when working in the woods. Here are the most common risks we've encountered, and wasy to stay safe.

In no particular order:
  • Poison Ivy
  • Random holes dug by Stevie
  • Partially buried barbed wire
  • Ticks
  • Mosquitos
  • Herbicides
  • Falling branches and trees
  • Power tool noise
  • Wild rose bushes (evil)
What to do:
  1. Watch where you are walking
  2. Check for ticks regularly
  3. Avoid areas with known poison ivy (good luck)
  4. Wear protective clothing
  5. Use insect repellent
  6. Use hearing protection with power tools
